python中zip的用法

##代码示例

a=[1,2,3]
b=[4,5,6]
print(“{}”.format(list(zip(a,b))))
print(“{}”.format({n:v for n,v in zip(a,b)}))

##结果
[(1, 4), (2, 5), (3, 6)]
{1: 4, 2: 5, 3: 6}
